DESCRIPTION OF PARAMETERS IN THE MODELS

a - number of bites per mosquito per unit of time
CFR - Case fatality ratio
DELTA - Per capita rate at which an individual leaves the infectious group, either to recovered or death
EPSILON - Per capita rate at which an exposed individual becomes infectious
LAMBDA - Force of infection
MU - Background birth/death/emigration/imigration rate
OMEGA - Preference factor at which mosquitos bite infectious individuals over others
PI - Chance of infection occuring upon an infectious bite
SIGMA - Per capita rate at which a recovered individual becomes susceptible again (i.e. loss of immunity)
TAU - Proportion of mosquitos that die upon contact with insecticide
THETA - Proportion of mosquitos that contact insecticide
TotalPop - Total population
